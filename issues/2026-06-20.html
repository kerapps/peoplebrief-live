✎ HR Tip of the Day

Facilitate an open meeting where each team can deflate one 'sacred cow' — a project or process everyone does because 'we’ve always done it that way.' It encourages innovation and trims inefficiencies, nurturing fresh perspectives on roles. The potential surprise? Some obsolete practices may be fortress-sized and fiercely protected, so handle with care.
