✎ HR Tip of the Day

This week, try a visibility audit: check how often managers meet directly with their teams, not just through digital dashboards or reports. Increasing face-to-face interactions, even virtually, can reveal gaps between perceived and real engagement. It’s the real presence that builds trust and conveys transparency, something data alone can't show.
